Travel & Lodging

✈️ Closest Airports

  • Fresno Yosemite (FAT) ~2.5 hours to Valley floor. Most convenient access vector.
  • San Francisco (SFO) ~4 hours to Valley floor. Best international flight options.
  • Oakland (OAK) ~3.5 hours to Valley floor. Less congested traffic pattern.
  • Sacramento (SMF) ~3.5 hours to Valley floor. Alternative northern path.

🏨 Lodging

  • Inside the Park: The Ahwahnee, Yosemite Valley Lodge, or Wawona Hotel. Higher cost, but less commute. Book early.
  • Outside the Park (Mariposa): The sweet spot for economic hotels and cabins. Scalable options.
  • Transit Latencies:
    • Mariposa to Glacier Point: ~1 hr 20 min
    • Mariposa to El Capitan Picnic: ~1 hr

🚗 Navigation

Cell service drops to near-zero once inside the granite portals. Download offline Google Maps vectors for the entire region before departing.

Park Checklist

🌲 Entry Regulations

Park entrance runs $20-$35 dollars per vehicle. Ensure you check the official NPS gateway to verify system passes for your exact arrival window.

👟 What to Pack

  • • Sturdy structural shoes / trail runners
  • • Layered apparel for microclimates
  • • High-efficiency sunscreen & UV shields
  • • Lots of water!

🌦️ Expected Climate

Late June weather transitions rapidly. Valley floors scale between 80°F–90°F. High elevations like Glacier Point chill significantly, lingering near 50°F–60°F early in the morning.

Make a Weekend of It

For those extending their operations into a mini-vacation, prioritize these high-value geographic sites within the park bounds:

📍 Valley & Tunnel View

The definitive structural landscape overview. Optimal photography window occurs at sunset when light breaks cleanly across El Capitan and Half Dome.

📍 Glacier Point & Bridalveil

Glacier Point drops a sheer 3,200-foot visualization directly down to the valley base. Bridalveil is easily accessible via a brief, high-throughput walking path.

📍 Mariposa Grove

Located near the south portal. Home to more than 500 mature giant sequoias. An great option for low-impact foot transit through ancient ecosystems.
